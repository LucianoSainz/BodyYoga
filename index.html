BodyYoga is a unique hot yoga series that combines the precision of therapeutic yoga and the foundations of traditional yoga in a specially heated room. In addition to the signature hatha-based Modo series, there are also Modo Flow and Power Flow classes in which the sequence flows through an invigorating yet accessible vinyasa yoga style. Then, slow down and enjoy our Yin and Meditation classes or step outside yoga for challenging barre and movement classes. There's something for everyone, and a growing community to connect with! Our Modo instructors each have a minimum of 500 hours training, and most have multiple certifications across various styles and disciplines rooted in personal practice. Whether you are an absolute beginner or a more experienced student, we offer a range of accessible classes that are challenging, healthy and fun.

Better known as Handstand Paula, is a beloved yoga teacher at BodyYoga Miami Beach for the past 10 years. Yogis love Paula’s classes for their strong sequencing, motivational + uplifting messages, fabulous playlists, challenging workout, core work and positivity + inspiration.


KiKi took her first Yoga class in California. She was introduced to vinyasa flow with a heavy focus on alignment. In Miami she completed a strict 200+hr training according to a traditional style from Mysore, India. That same year, she traveled to India to expand her practice. As KiKi continued into her next 200hr training she gained appreciation for Yoga from a more modern perspective. Teaching over the past few years in Miami has given students from all backgrounds a chance to discover KiKi’s warm, fun and challenging classes.
